Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

Use the Forgot Password page, then enter OTP on Reset Password.

Where can I track my order?

Open Order Tracking and enter your order ID from purchase confirmation.

How do I request a refund?

Use Returns & Refund Request with your order ID and reason.

How can I contact support quickly?

Create a ticket with priority marked as high for urgent issues.
